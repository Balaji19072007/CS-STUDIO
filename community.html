Community Guidelines

Be Respectful, Be Helpful

Our community thrives when everyone follows these simple guidelines.

Do's

  • Be respectful and inclusive to all members
  • Provide helpful and constructive feedback
  • Share knowledge and resources
  • Use appropriate language and tone
  • Give credit when using others' work

Don'ts

  • Post spam or self-promotional content
  • Share complete solutions to coding problems
  • Use offensive or discriminatory language
  • Harass or bully other members
  • Share personal information without consent

By participating in our community, you agree to follow these guidelines. Violations may result in temporary or permanent restrictions from community features.
